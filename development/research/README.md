# Research & investigation artifacts

Studies, comparisons, and design research that **inform** — and often become — the
plans in [`../planning/`](../planning/). These are the record of *how* a decision
was reached: not action items, and not the authoritative spec.

Once a study produces a plan, **the plan in `../planning/` is the source of
truth**; the research doc stays here as the rationale/history (keep it even after
the plan ships — it explains the "why").

Distinct from [`../pending/`](../pending/), which holds *open action items* not yet
turned into a plan.
