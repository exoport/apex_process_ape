# Pending items

Open investigations, unresolved questions, and TODOs **not yet turned into a
plan**. When a pending item becomes a plan it moves to
[`../planning/`](../planning/), and its rationale/research moves to
[`../research/`](../research/); resolved or superseded items are removed.

This folder is for *pending things* only — not finished research (that's
[`../research/`](../research/)) and not authoritative specs (that's
[`../planning/`](../planning/)).
